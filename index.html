I am a faculty member in the Department of Microbiology and Plant Pathology at the University of California, Riverside. My research integrates artificial intelligence, machine learning (AI/ML), and nano-scale molecular modeling to investigate disease mechanisms and advance bioinformatics analyses. By combining computational modeling with large-scale biological data, my work aims to improve disease prediction, classification, and therapeutic discovery and delivery.
