Assistant Professor in AI/Machine learning and Molecular modeling

I am a faculty member in the Department of Microbiology and Plant Pathology at University of California Riverside.

My research integrates artificial intelligence, machine learning (AI/ML), and nano-scale molecular modeling to investigate disease mechanisms and advance bioinformatics analyses. By combining computational modeling with large-scale biological data, my work aims to improve disease prediction, classification, and therapeutic discovery and delivery.

Research

  • Using AI and machine learning for disease modeling in both agriculture and human health
  • Computational modeling and molecular dynamics (MD) simulations of complex biological systems
  • Biopharmaceutical, pharmaceutical engineering, agriculture and plant applications
  • Multiscale modeling and simulations
