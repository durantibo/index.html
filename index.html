I am a faculty member in the Department of Microbiology and Plant Pathology at the University of California, Riverside. My research integrates artificial intelligence, machine learning (AI/ML), and nano-scale molecular modeling to investigate disease mechanisms and advance bioinformatics analyses.

UCR Faculty Profile

Research

  • AI/machine learning in agriculture to model and forecast plant diseases
  • Computational modeling and molecular dynamics (MD) simulations of complex biological systems
  • Integrated data-driven and physics-based approaches for disease understanding, prediction, and therapy development
  • Interdisciplinary research spanning agriculture, plant pathology, biopharmaceutical science, biophysics, and chemical engineering
